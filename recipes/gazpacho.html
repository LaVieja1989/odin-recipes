Gazpachito

Directions

  1. Dump all the ingredients except the vinegar and the black pepper in a blender (peel the cucumber and the garlic and dispose the pepper and tomato stems before, obviously).
  2. Blend thoroughly. When finish, add the black pepper and vinegar to your taste.
  3. Leave the gazpacho in the fridge for an hour or so.
  4. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 2 green peppers.
  • 3 ripe tomatoes.
  • 1 cucumber.
  • Two teasppons of olive oil.
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • vinegar
  • Garlic
