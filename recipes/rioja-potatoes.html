Rioja Style Potatoes <3

Directions

  1. Peel the potatoes and dice them in cubic chunks. In an olied pot, add the potatoes and fry softly for 3 minutes.
  2. Add the paprika and the salt and keep moving the potatoes to mix.
  3. Add water to cover and the chorizo in 2 inches slices.
  4. Add one or two bay leaves and let it boil for 30 minutes.
  5. Enjoy with some ball peppers!

Ingredients

  • 3 generous potatoes.
  • 1 chorizo sausage.
  • Sweet paprika.
  • Two teasppons of olive oil.
  • Salt
  • Bay leaves.
  • Ball peppers.
